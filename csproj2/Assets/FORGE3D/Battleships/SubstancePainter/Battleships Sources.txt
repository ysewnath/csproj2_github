[FORGE3D] BATTLESHIPS Substance Painter Sources
-----------------------------------------------

* Why there are no Substance Painter source files included?

Unfortunately the size of the Unity package has grown over 1+ Gb with all Substance Sources, so we decided to move the sources away from the package.

* Where do I get Substance Painter sources?

You can get the download link by sending a direct request with your Order Id (https://assetstore.unity.com/orders) at support@forge3d.com
